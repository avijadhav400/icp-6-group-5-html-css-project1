Diamond Membership

The Diamond Membership at Fitness Plus gives you elite-level access to the facilities and our coaching services. This membership is for anyone looking to make a life changing transformation in body and mind over a 3-month period with the total support of Fitness Plus. This all-inclusive membership leaves no stone unturned and is a guaranteed transformational result.
What’s Included?

______

3 Months of full 24/7 Platinum Access

______
You will get 24/7 access to the gym for the full 3-month period of your membership, allowing you to train whenever you want.

30 minute Nutritional Development session every week

______
To develop your understanding of nutrition and allow our coaches to review, progress and refine your current diet.

1 Hour exercise based personal training session every week

______
Where you will undergo a structural assessment, biomechanics test and be given a programme to follow in your own personal gym sessions.

Full exercise and nutrition-based consultation

______
To allow our coaches to gain a full understanding of your goals, habits, lifestyle factors and experience. We can then begin creating a bespoke training plan and nutrition programme based around your needs.

Access to our learning resources

______
Such as our eBook series, commercial eating guides, Facebook/WhatsApp groups and 24/7 access to your coach.

Merchandise/Supplement package

______
This includes a Fitness Plus logbook, shaker bottle, 1kg of Whey and a 500g Creatine.

Plus 15% discount on ALL products and supplements throughout your membership. AND 1 Year subscription to MyFitnessPal premium service

The total for this 3 month Diamond package is £840 inc VAT
